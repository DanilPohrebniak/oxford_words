"Oxford words" - a program designed to enhance your language skills!
Word Match Trainer presents you with a set of eight words: four in English and four in Russian.
Your task is to match the corresponding words correctly. As you progress through the exercises and provide accurate
answers, your rating will increase, reflecting your proficiency in word matching.

The program offers an engaging and interactive experience, challenging you to recall and associate words in different
languages. With each correct match, you'll not only gain confidence in your language abilities but also see your
rating climb higher.
